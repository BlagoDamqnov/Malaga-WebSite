DESCRIPTION

Learning to end hunger is a single player offline game, developed by students in 11-th grade.
The focus of the game is on teaching kids and even their parents about basic facts about the
lifestyle of people living in poverty. By answering more than the half of the questions correctly your game character will get fed,
if not - he will stay hungry until you win the game.
